Our Services

At Aotea Auto, we offer a range of professional detailing services to keep your vehicle looking its best:

  • Exterior Detailing: Hand wash, wax, wheel cleaning, and paint protection.
  • Interior Detailing: Deep vacuum, shampoo, dashboard and leather treatment.
  • Paint Correction: Remove swirl marks, light scratches, and restore gloss.
  • Upholstery Deep Cleaning: Fabric, carpet, and seat steam and shampoo clean.
  • Dura-Seal Application: Long-lasting surface and fabric protection.
