Dura-Seal Ceramic Protection

Industry-leading ceramic coatings for long-lasting shine and protection.

Dura-Seal Ultra

A thicker, longer-lasting nano-ceramic coating that bonds to paint for high-gloss, advanced UV and contaminant protection. Ultra is designed for maximum durability, with an expected lifespan of up to 8 years.
